# Full Description

Social Soil is a cozy strategy game about restoring land from the ground up.

Plant beans, maize, squash, and fungi. Guide mycelium networks across the farm, harvest when crops mature, and keep nutrients moving through a living soil system. As the world opens up, villagers, farmers, baskets, banks, and markets create a local economy where food and resources need to flow.

Choose Tutorial to learn the loop, then take on Challenge mode to chase higher scores and ranks. Read the soil, place plants carefully, protect growth from hungry visitors, and build a resilient network before resources run dry.

Features:

- Farm ecosystem play with crops, mushrooms, and nutrient bars
- Myco network placement for connected growth and resource movement
- Village trade phase with farmers, baskets, shops, banks, and social agents
- Score, rank, and high-score progression
- Touch-friendly drag-and-drop controls
- Original pixel-art inspired farming world

Social Soil was created with Grassroots Economics as a playful way to explore regenerative systems, cooperation, and circular local exchange.
